FUTURE OF DIGITAL TV

This 8-month project is brought to you by five students from the
‍Master of Human-Computer Interaction program at Carnegie Mellon University.

In this client-sponsored project, we worked with the company InterDigital to speculate and design a future digital television experience that can be expected to emerge in the next three to five years.

InterDigital specializes in research and development of novel video communication technologies, with a particular focus on utilizing synthetic content, so our task was to unearth the unsatisfied user needs in current TV viewing experiences and better future experiences by leveraging synthetic content.
